Biological integrator neuron — delayed coincidence

Spike trains arrive from feature neurons A and B. Axons impose conduction delays (K). A distal dendrite votes binary only when delayed spikes coincide over a hard threshold. Proximal V adds weighted drive. The soma does leaky integrate-and-fire.
